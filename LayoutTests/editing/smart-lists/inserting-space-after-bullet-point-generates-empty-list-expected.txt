Typing "* A" + Enter + "* " with no text after the second marker should still produce an unordered list where the second list item is empty (contains a placeholder br).

|   class="Apple-disc-list"
|   style="list-style-type: disc;"
|   <li>
|     "A"
|     <br>
|   <li>
|     <#selection-caret>
|     <br>
